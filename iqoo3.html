Product Details


Display and Camera Vivo iQOO 3 sports a 6.44 inches(16.36 cm) bezel-less display with a screen resolution of 1080x2400 pixels and a pixel density of 409ppi, ensure crisp display to its users. The vibrant display of the device is enhanced by a super AMOLED, 2.5D curved glass. The iQOO3 gets a quad-camera setup with a 48MP primary wide-angle PDAF camera. Backing the primary lens are two similar 13MP cameras and a 2MP depth-sensing camera. The front camera gets a 16MP lens embedded in the punch-hole design. The other features of the camera include a 20x digital zoom output, a 120-degree field of view sensor, and bokeh shots. Performance and Battery Powering the Vivo iQOO 3 is a Qualcomm Snapdragon 865 octa-core processor backed by Adreno 650 GPU to enhance seamless user experience. Additionally, the phone showcases 6GB RAM for lag-free functioning. The total capacity of a battery in the iQOO3 stands at 4,440 mAh. To ensure increased productivity throughout the day, the device also supports super-fast charging facility, refilling 50% of the battery in 15 minutes. Storage and Connectivity The handset showcases an internal storage space of 128GB, which cannot be expanded further. The operating system functions on Android 10 (Q), customed by Funtouch OS UI. The network connections provided by this handset will be 5G network support amongst the 2G,3G, 4G VoLTE. Other connectivity features include wifi connectivity of 802.11, a/ac/ax/b/g/n/n 5GHz, Mobile Hotspot, Bluetooth 5.0 and GPS.
