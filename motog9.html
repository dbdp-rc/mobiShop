Moto G9 is a stunning splashproof design considering its price range. Starting from 4GB RAM, 5000mAh Turbo Power Charging technology to impressive camera setup users get it all. However, the hybrid SIM slot might be a cause of dissatisfaction among the dual SIM users, considering the fact that the smartphone has an internal storage space of 64GB. Budget-friendly device with spectacular features Display and Camera Moto G9 features a 6.5-inch IPS LCD display, with a screen resolution of 720 x 1600 pixels and a pixel density of 270 ppi. The smartphone has a bezel-less waterdrop notch display and an aspect ratio of 20:9. Further, the screen to body ratio of the device is 87%. The smartphone offers a stunning triple camera set up, consisting of a 48MP f/1.7 Primary Camera, a 2MP f/2.4 camera for depth sensing and another 2MP f/2.4 Camera. Some of the rear camera features include ISO control, HDR mode, Face Detection, Exposure Compensation, Touch to focus, and Digital Zoom. On the front, users get access to an 8MP f/2.2 Selfie Shooter. Configuration and Battery Moto G9 is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 662 chipset, along with an Octa-core Kyro 260 Quad-core processor framework running at a clock speed of 2GHz and 1.8GHz. It also features a 4GB RAM and Adreno 610 GPU. The device gets its power from a non-replaceable 5000mAh Li-ion battery, equipped with 20W Turbo Power Charging technology.
