Product Details


Gionee Max is an impressive smartphone offering a variety of stunning specs, the massive 5000mAh battery equipped with reverse charging technology can be considered as an astonishing feature within such a price range. Further, the 32GB internal storage, decent RAM and processor setup along with face unlock support offered by the device, turns it into an amazing deal to invest in. Overall, Gionee Max can be termed as an allrounder device offering multiple basic amenities that a customer would need. Budget device equipped with multiple specs Display and Camera Gionee Max has a 6.1-inch HD+ IPS LCD display. The smartphone has a screen resolution of 720 x 1560 pixels accompanied with a 2.5D Curved Glass. Further, its bezel-less waterdrop notch display has a pixel density of 282ppi. The smartphone presents a 13MP rear camera, equipped with features like Auto Focus, Exposure compensation, Slow motion, ISO control, Digital Zoom, Face Detection, Face Beauty, Audio Note, HDR mode, Bokeh Mode and Touch to focus. On the front, there is a 5MP selfie camera. Configuration and Battery Gionee Max is powered by Unisoc SC9863A chipset and an Octa-core Cortex A55 1.6GHz processor. Its 2GB RAM s responsible for offering smooth performance. The smartphone derives its energy from a 5000mAh Li-Polymer battery. Which can be replaced by the users in case they notice any type of malfunctions. Further, the smartphone offers 28 days of standby time in case of 2G network usage. The best part about the device is it is equipped with advanced reverse charging technology. Storage and Connectivity Gionee Max offers 32GB internal storage space which can be expanded up to 256 GB. Connectivity-wise the smartphone supports 4GVoLTE, Bluetooth v4.2, Mobile Hotspot, MicroUSB 2.0, A-GPS and Wi-Fi.
