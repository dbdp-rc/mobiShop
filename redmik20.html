Product Details


The Xiaomi Redmi K20 Pro is a great device, which has all the features that one desires to have in mid-range. The strong configuration is capable to handle multiple tasks simultaneously without any issue. Be it graphics-centric games or high multimedia consumption, the device does not compromise in any field. Coming to the camera front, it produces detailed and bright pictures as one expects from the Chinese giant. Giving a prolonged usage experience with the high capacity of a battery, it only lacks storage expansion. Experience the premium in mid-range Display and Design The Xiaomi Redmi K20 Pro bears a 6.39-inch AMOLED display, which comes with a complete bezel-less finish that renders panorama view along with a stylish aspect ratio of 19.5:9. It has Corning Gorilla Glass above the display that acts as a shield against bruises. A screen resolution of 1,080 x 2,340 pixels enhances the visual experience with a sharp pixel density of 403 PPI. Performance Driving the system, there is an octa-core Kryo 485 processor setup (a 2.84GHz single-core, a 2.42GHz tri-core and a 1.8GHz quad-core), which is seated on a Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 chip and delivers a powerful performance. A 6GB RAM and an Adreno 640 GPU handle heavy multitasking and games that demands good graphical effects. Battery and Camera The Xiaomi Redmi K20 Pro is equipped with a 4,000mAh Li-Polymer battery, which keeps the entertainment going on for long hours. Also, the device supports QC4+ fast charge protocol that assures up to 58% charging in just 30 minutes. Speaking on the optics front, there is a triple rear camera set up having a 48MP main lens, 13MP super wide-angle sensor and an 8MP telephoto lens. The combination is capable of clicking mesmerizing images with bright and detailed finish. For the selfie freaks, there is a 20MP front camera, which can record full HD videos at 30 fps speed. Storage and Connectivity Moving to the storage department, it houses a 128GB non-expandable storage. The Xiaomi Redmi K20 Pro has a wide range of connectivity options including 4G with VoLTE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth v5.0, GPS, USB type-C, Mobile Hotspot.
